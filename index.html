About

she / her — Digital Artist & Portrait Specialist

Hi! I'm KOSHDI — a digital artist specializing in expressive black-and-white illustration, character studies, and celebrity portraits. My work blends clean linework with atmospheric rendering, capturing personality and emotion in every piece.

I've been drawing for over 3 years in digital, with a passion for portrait work and character design. I'm constantly inspired by cinema, music, and pop culture. Every commission is an opportunity to grow and refine my craft.
